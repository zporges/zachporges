Minecraft Mods

Mods are modifications to the game of minecraft. They can make the game alot better or harder. They can make you VERY overpowered. A youtuber SSundee does alot of mod reviews, check him out.

Some mods add:

  • Blocks
  • Bools
  • Rockets to the moon
  • ect.

Mods can be a lot of fun, but if you put a bunch of mods together, you get a modpack. Modpacks can have over 100 mods! They can make different mods that do two different things work together and make something awesome.
